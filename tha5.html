THE POPULAR PLACE IN CHENNAI

Marina Beach

Marina is a natural urban beach in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India, along the Bay of Bengal.

Fort St. George

Fort St. George is a fortress at the coastal city of Chennai, India. Founded in 1639

Parthasarathy Temple

A beautiful 8th-century temple dedicated to Lord Vishnu

San Thome Basilica

A stunning church built over the tomb of St. Thomas, one of Jesus' apostles

Anna Centenary Library

One of the largest libraries in Asia, a paradise for book lovers

Government Museum, Chennai

A treasure trove of art and history, featuring works by Raja Ravi Varma

Connemara Public Library

One of India's four National Depository Libraries, housing century-old publications and serving as a depository for the United Nations

Thiruveedhi Amman Temple

A revered temple located in Arumbakkam, known for its spiritual significance
